Featured Rescue

This is Oscar. He was rescued from an abandoned house in Old Town 10 days ago. He is 3 years old, 75 lbs. and overwhelmingly loveable to be around. He would make a great addition to any family, or companion for any single individual. He does well in apartment dwellings but is a fabulous fit for residential homes with a good amount of green lawn space, so he is able to play and run around for a great workout. If you are interested in adopting Oscar, please give us a call at, (706) 221-5461, or visit us at The Augusta Animal Shelter located on, 5478 Rescue Drive, Augusta, GA 30901.
